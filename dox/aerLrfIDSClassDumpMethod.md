**Dump** invokes the [DSDUMP](DSDUMP.html) opcode. 

```
 *ids* . **Dump()** 
```

### Remarks
**Dump** invokes the [DSDUMP](DSDUMP.html) opcode to dump the contents of the fields of the **data structure** assembled into a string.
